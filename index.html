ABOUT US

"Our company has two directors, Jack Quinton and Mike Brennan."

We are both experienced in a large variety of electrical expertise from small residential installations to large commercial project management. With over 15 years experience in the industry we have the knowledge to fulfill all your electrical requirements.

At QB Electrical we believe communication is paramount and due to the size of our company you will be dealing with the same tradesmen doing the work from start to finish. As registered electricians we will provide you with Certificates of Compliance and Electrical Safety certificates at the completion of work.

We welcome the opportunity to be of service to you and count you as one of our valued customers.

All customers can receive a 2% discount for early patment.
